Dola is the marketplace where AI Agents are built, bought, and put to work — all in one place. Whether you're a creator who wants to monetize your expertise by building and selling Agents, or a buyer who needs a capable AI Agent without the setup headache, Dola gives you everything you need from day one.

## Who Dola Is For

**Creators** design and publish AI Agents on the Dola marketplace. You bring the idea, the persona, and the instructions — Dola handles model access, payments, and delivery. If you have knowledge, a workflow, or a use case worth packaging, you can turn it into a product that earns revenue while you sleep.

**Buyers** browse the marketplace and purchase ready-to-work Agents. The moment you complete a purchase, your Agent is activated and ready to use. No deployment, no API keys to manage, no integration friction — just open it and start working.

## What Makes Dola Different

### Model Freedom
You choose the AI model that powers your Agent from a curated selection of top-tier models. Dola also includes initial traffic costs with every Agent, so buyers can try an Agent before they commit — lowering the barrier to that first sale.

### More Than Just Agents
The Dola marketplace isn't limited to AI Agents. Creators can also list personal services, custom configurations, and individual Skills. If it adds value to someone's workflow, you can sell it on Dola.

### Built-In Referral System
Every user gets a referral link. Share it, and earn a commission every time someone makes a purchase through your link. The referral system is built directly into the platform — no third-party tools, no manual tracking.

### End-to-End Commercialization
Dola covers the entire lifecycle: you create an Agent, list it on the marketplace, Dola delivers it to the buyer, and then settles your earnings — all within a single platform. There's no need to stitch together separate tools for building, selling, and getting paid.
