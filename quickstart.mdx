---
title: "Dola Quickstart: Buy or Publish Your First AI Agent"
sidebarTitle: "Quickstart"
description: "Create your Dola account, purchase a ready-made AI Agent from the marketplace, or configure and publish your own Agent for sale in minutes."
---

Dola gets you from zero to working AI Agent in minutes — whether you want to buy one or build one. Follow the path that fits your goal: pick up a ready-made Agent from the marketplace, or step into the Creator Dashboard and publish your own.

<Note>
  Initial traffic costs are included with every Agent on Dola. Buyers get real usage from the moment they activate, and creators can let buyers try the Agent before committing to a purchase — no upfront risk on either side.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buy an Agent">
    Follow these steps to find the right Agent, purchase it, and put it to work immediately.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your Dola account">
        Go to [dolaio.cn](https://dolaio.cn) and sign up for a free account. You only need an email address to get started. Once you confirm your email, you'll land directly in the marketplace.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse the marketplace">
        Explore Agents by category, use case, or keyword. Use the filters in the left sidebar to narrow results by capability, model, or price. Each listing shows the Agent's persona, what it can do, and the Skills it comes with.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Purchase an Agent">
        Open an Agent's listing page to read its full description, included Skills, and pricing. When you're ready, click **Buy Now** and complete checkout. Dola handles payment processing — there's nothing to configure on your end.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect and activate">
        After purchase, the Agent appears in your **My Agents** dashboard. Click **Activate** and the Agent is immediately live. Start your first conversation or task right away — no API keys, no deployment steps, no waiting.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize to your workflow (optional)">
        Once activated, you can adjust the Agent's settings, update its instructions to match your style, or add extra Skills from the marketplace. Customization is always optional — the Agent works out of the box as the creator configured it.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Not sure which Agent to pick? Sort the marketplace by **Top Rated** or **Most Popular** to see what other buyers are using most. Agent listings include sample interactions so you can preview behavior before purchasing.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Create and Sell an Agent">
    Follow these steps to build your Agent, publish it to the marketplace, and start earning revenue.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create your Dola account">
        Go to [dolaio.cn](https://dolaio.cn) and sign up. Creator features are available to all registered users — no separate application required.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the Creator Dashboard">
        From your account menu, navigate to **Creator Dashboard**, then click **New Agent**. This opens the Agent builder where you'll configure everything from persona to pricing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure your Agent">
        Fill in the essentials:
        - **Name** — give your Agent a clear, memorable identity.
        - **Description** — explain what the Agent does and who it's for. This appears on your marketplace listing.
        - **Persona** — define the Agent's tone, style, and character so every interaction feels consistent.
        - **Instructions** — write the system-level guidance that shapes how the Agent responds and behaves.

        Strong configuration here directly improves buyer satisfaction and your ratings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select an AI model">
        Choose the underlying AI model that powers your Agent from Dola's supported model list. Each model has different strengths — pick the one that best fits your Agent's purpose. Initial traffic costs are covered by Dola, so buyers can start using the Agent right after purchase without any extra charges landing on you.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Skills (optional)">
        Skills are modular capabilities you can attach to your Agent — things like web search, image generation, or custom API calls. Browse the Skills library and add any that extend your Agent's value. You can also sell Skills separately on the marketplace.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set your price and publish">
        Enter your listing price, review the preview of your marketplace page, and click **Publish**. Your Agent goes live on the Dola marketplace immediately. Dola handles delivery and payment processing for every sale automatically.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Share your referral link">
        After publishing, grab your personal referral link from the **Creator Dashboard**. Share it on social media, in your newsletter, or with your community. Every purchase made through your link earns you an additional commission on top of your Agent's sale price.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Write your Agent's description for the buyer, not for yourself. Focus on outcomes — what will they be able to accomplish? Listings that lead with specific use cases consistently outperform generic ones.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
