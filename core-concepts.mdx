Dola is built around a small set of interlocking concepts. Once you understand how each piece fits together — what an Agent is, how Skills extend it, how the Marketplace connects creators to buyers, and how payments and referrals flow — the entire platform becomes intuitive. This page walks through each concept clearly so you can use Dola with confidence.

---

## Agent

An Agent is a configurable AI-powered digital worker. Creators define the Agent's name, persona, and instructions, then select an AI model to power it. Buyers purchase an Agent from the marketplace and activate it instantly — no setup required. From that moment, the Agent is ready to receive tasks, answer questions, or run workflows exactly as the creator designed it.

Think of an Agent as a specialist you can put to work the moment you hire them.

---

## Skill

A Skill is a modular capability that extends what an Agent can do. Skills plug into an Agent to unlock specific actions — for example, searching the web, generating images, calling external APIs, or processing documents. Creators add Skills during Agent configuration, and buyers can sometimes add extra Skills after purchase.

Skills can also be listed and sold independently on the marketplace, making them a standalone product for creators who want to package a specific capability without building a full Agent around it.

---

## Model

Every Agent runs on an underlying AI model — the engine that processes language, reasons through problems, and generates responses. Dola supports a curated selection of top-tier models, and creators choose which model best fits their Agent's purpose during setup.

Different models have different strengths: some excel at structured reasoning, others at creative writing or code generation. Choosing the right model is part of how creators shape the quality and character of their Agent.

<Note>
  Dola covers initial traffic costs for every Agent. This means buyers can start using an Agent right after purchase without any hidden charges, and creators can offer a genuine first experience without worrying about covering that usage themselves.
</Note>

---

## Marketplace

The Marketplace is the Dola storefront — the place where creators list their products and buyers discover and purchase them. Listings can include fully configured Agents, individual Skills, custom configurations, and personal services offered by creators.

The Marketplace is organized by category and use case, making it easy for buyers to find exactly what they need. Every listing shows pricing, included Skills, the model in use, and a description written by the creator.

---

## Creator

A Creator is any Dola user who builds and publishes products on the Marketplace. Creators design Agents, write personas and instructions, select models, bundle Skills, and set prices. Once a listing is live, Dola handles delivery and payment — creators focus on building great products, not managing transactions.

Creators earn revenue from every sale and can multiply that income through the built-in referral system.

---

## Buyer

A Buyer is any Dola user who purchases an Agent, Skill, or service from the Marketplace. After completing a purchase, the product is activated immediately — no configuration, no waiting period, no technical steps required unless the buyer chooses to customize further.

Buyers can hold multiple Agents in their account, each serving a different purpose or use case.

---

## Referral & Commission

Every Dola user — creator or buyer — has a personal referral link. When someone makes a purchase through your link, you earn a commission on that sale. The commission system is built directly into the platform; there's nothing extra to install or configure.

Referrals are one of the primary growth engines on Dola. Creators who share their referral links actively earn more than their base listing revenue, and buyers can earn commissions simply by recommending Agents they already use and love.

---

## Settlement

Settlement is how Dola handles the financial side of every transaction. When a buyer completes a purchase, Dola processes the payment and queues the creator's earnings for distribution. Creators receive their revenue through the platform's built-in settlement system — no need to set up a separate payment processor or manage invoices manually.

Settlement runs automatically in the background, so creators can focus on building and buyers can focus on getting work done.

---

## How It All Fits Together

<Tip>
  Dola is designed as a single, closed loop: a Creator builds an Agent, selects a Model, adds Skills, and lists it on the Marketplace. A Buyer finds it, purchases it, and activates it instantly. Dola processes the payment and settles the Creator's earnings automatically. Referral links spread the word and generate commissions along the way. Every concept on this page plays a role in that loop — and every role is handled within one platform.
</Tip>
