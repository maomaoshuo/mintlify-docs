Getting started on Dola takes just a few minutes. Whether you're here to discover and purchase AI Agents or to build and sell your own, this guide walks you through every step — from creating your account to being fully ready for the marketplace.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Go to dolaio.cn and click Sign Up">
    Open your browser and navigate to [dolaio.cn](https://dolaio.cn). On the homepage, click the **Sign Up** button in the top-right corner to begin the registration process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your credentials or use a social login">
    Enter your email address and create a strong password, then click **Create Account**. Alternatively, you can sign in with a supported social login provider — simply click the corresponding button on the sign-up screen and follow the authentication prompts from that provider.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Dola sends a confirmation link to the email address you provided. Open that email and click the **Verify Email** button. You must complete this step before you can access the marketplace or publish any content.

    <Note>
      If the verification email doesn't appear within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. You can also request a new link from the login screen — see [FAQ](/account/faq) for details.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After verifying your email, Dola prompts you to fill in your profile details:

    - **Display name** — the name buyers and other users will see across the platform
    - **Avatar** — upload a clear photo or logo that represents you or your brand
    - **Bio** — write a short description of who you are and what kinds of Agents you build or use

    <Tip>
      Add a professional avatar and bio if you plan to sell — buyers frequently check creator profiles before making a purchase, and a well-written bio builds trust immediately.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your role">
    Select how you plan to use Dola:

    - **Buyer** — you want to discover and purchase AI Agents built by creators
    - **Creator** — you want to build, list, and sell AI Agents on the marketplace
    - **Both** — you want to do both simultaneously

    <Note>
      You can switch between buyer and creator mode at any time from your profile settings, so don't worry about locking yourself in at this stage.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a payout method (creators only)">
    If you selected Creator or Both, set up your payout method now so you can receive earnings as soon as your first sale comes in. Navigate to **Account Settings → Payments** and follow the prompts to connect your preferred payout option. For detailed guidance on payment schedules and supported methods, see [Settlements](/payments/settlements).
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're ready — explore the marketplace">
    Your account is fully configured. Head to the marketplace to browse available AI Agents, or click **Create Agent** in your dashboard to start building your first listing. The Dola community is waiting.
  </Step>

</Steps>
