Your Dola account settings give you full control over how you appear on the platform, how you're notified about activity, and how you keep your account secure. Access all settings by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner and selecting **Account Settings** from the dropdown menu.

---

## Profile Settings

Your profile is your public-facing identity on Dola. Buyers browsing the marketplace can visit your creator page to learn about you and review your published Agents, so keeping your profile accurate and up to date directly supports your credibility as a creator.

- **Display name** — update the name shown on your listings, reviews, and throughout the platform
- **Avatar** — upload or replace your profile image; square images at least 256×256 px work best
- **Bio** — edit your short description to reflect your expertise, background, or the kinds of Agents you specialize in building
- **Public profile URL** — your unique creator page link (e.g., `dolaio.cn/@yourname`), visible to any buyer who views your listings
- **Social links** — optionally add links to your GitHub, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, or personal website; these appear on your creator profile and help buyers learn more about you

To update any of these, go to **Account Settings → Profile** and save your changes.

## Notification Preferences

Dola keeps you informed about activity that matters to you through both email and in-app notifications. You can choose exactly which events trigger a notification and through which channel.

**Email notifications** — Dola can send you email alerts for:
- A new sale of one of your Agents
- A new review posted on your listing
- A payout successfully processed to your account
- Marketplace announcements and platform updates

**In-app notifications** — displayed inside the Dola dashboard in real time:
- Messages received from buyers
- Status updates for Agents you've submitted for review or recently published

To manage your preferences, go to **Account Settings → Notifications** and toggle each notification type on or off individually. Changes take effect immediately.

## Payment & Payout Settings

All billing and payout activity is managed from **Account Settings → Payments**.

**For creators:**
- Add or update your payout method to ensure earnings are sent to the right account
- Review upcoming and past payouts, including amounts and processing dates
- For a full explanation of payout schedules, fees, and minimum thresholds, see [Settlements](/payments/settlements)

**For buyers:**
- View your complete billing history, including past purchases and invoices
- Manage active Agent subscriptions — you can cancel or modify a subscription at any time from this section
- Update the payment method on file for future purchases

<Note>
  If you use Dola as both a buyer and a creator, both billing history and payout information are available in the same Payments section, organized under separate tabs.
</Note>

## Security

Protecting your Dola account is especially important if you have active listings and earnings. Dola provides several tools to help you stay secure.

**Change your password**

Go to **Account Settings → Security → Change Password**. Enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm. Choose a password that is at least 12 characters long and unique to Dola.

**Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)**

Two-factor authentication adds a second layer of protection to your account and is strongly recommended for all creator accounts.

To set it up:
1. Go to **Account Settings → Security → Enable 2FA**
2. Scan the QR code displayed on screen using an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy
3. Enter the six-digit code shown in your app to confirm the setup
4. Save your backup codes in a secure location — you'll need them if you ever lose access to your authenticator app

**Review active sessions**

Under **Account Settings → Security → Active Sessions**, you can see a list of all devices and locations currently signed into your account. If you spot an unfamiliar session, click **Sign Out** next to it to revoke access immediately.

## Account Deletion

If you'd like to permanently close your Dola account, go to **Account Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account** and follow the confirmation steps.

<Warning>
  Deleting your account is irreversible. All of your active listings will be removed from the marketplace, your profile will no longer be accessible, and any pending earnings will be processed according to Dola's terms of service before the account is closed. Make sure to withdraw any available balance before initiating deletion.
</Warning>
