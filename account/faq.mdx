## General

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="What is Dola?">
    Dola is an AI Agent marketplace where creators build and publish specialized AI Agents, and buyers purchase or subscribe to those Agents to automate tasks, enhance workflows, and solve real problems. The platform handles discovery, payments, and distribution so creators can focus on building and buyers can focus on getting things done.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Dola for — do I need to be a developer to use it?">
    Dola is designed for a wide range of users. Buyers don't need any technical background — you simply browse the marketplace, purchase an Agent that fits your needs, and activate it in a few clicks. Creators benefit from having some familiarity with AI tools or workflows, but Dola also supports no-code approaches that make Agent creation accessible to non-developers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Dola available in my country?">
    Dola is available globally for browsing and purchasing Agents. Payout availability for creators may vary by region depending on supported payment providers. During account setup, the platform will indicate which payout methods are available in your location. If you have questions about availability in a specific country, reach out to Dola's support team.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Buying Agents

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="How do I activate an Agent after purchasing it?">
    After completing your purchase, go to your **Dashboard → My Agents** and find the Agent you just bought. Click **Activate** and follow any setup steps the creator has provided — these might include connecting the Agent to an external service or entering a configuration value. Once activated, the Agent is ready to use immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try an Agent before buying?">
    Some creators offer a free trial tier or a limited demo version of their Agent. Look for a **Try for Free** or **Demo** button on the listing page. If no trial is available, check the listing description carefully — many creators include screenshots, videos, or detailed use-case explanations to help you make an informed decision before purchasing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the refund policy?">
    Dola offers a refund window for eligible purchases — check the listing page and Dola's refund policy documentation for the exact conditions and time limits. Refund requests are evaluated case by case, and Agents that have been heavily used or are non-defective may not qualify. To request a refund, go to **Dashboard → My Agents → [Agent name] → Request Refund** and describe your reason.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a creator updates the Agent I purchased?">
    Updates to Agents you've already purchased are automatically available to you at no additional cost. When a creator publishes an update, you'll see a notification in your dashboard. Some updates apply instantly, while others may require you to re-activate or re-configure the Agent — the creator's update notes will explain what changed and what action, if any, you need to take.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Creating and Selling

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Do I need coding skills to create an Agent?">
    Not necessarily. Dola supports both code-based and no-code Agent creation. If you're comfortable with tools like prompt engineering, workflow builders, or visual automation platforms, you can create and publish an Agent without writing a single line of code. Developers who want to integrate custom logic or APIs can do so through the supported technical configuration options in the Agent editor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does listing review take?">
    Most Agent listings are reviewed within one to three business days. During peak submission periods it may take slightly longer. You'll receive an in-app notification and an email once the review is complete, letting you know whether your listing has been approved, needs changes, or has been rejected with feedback. To avoid delays, make sure your listing description, pricing, and category are complete and accurate before submitting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I increase my sales?">
    The most effective ways to grow your sales on Dola are to write a clear and detailed listing description, include a demo video or screenshots, respond promptly to buyer messages, and actively collect reviews from satisfied customers. Keeping your Agent updated and fixing reported issues quickly also builds your reputation over time. Creators with complete profiles and multiple published Agents tend to rank higher in marketplace search results.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I sell both an Agent and individual skills separately?">
    Yes. Dola allows creators to list a full Agent as a standalone product and also publish individual skills or components separately. This lets you offer both a complete solution for buyers who want everything in one package and modular building blocks for advanced users who want to customize their own setups. Manage each listing independently from your **Creator Dashboard**.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Payments

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="When do I receive my earnings?">
    Earnings from sales are typically made available for withdrawal on a rolling basis after a short clearing period, during which Dola processes the transaction and accounts for any applicable refund window. Once cleared, you can request a payout from **Account Settings → Payments → Withdraw**. For a full breakdown of the payout schedule, see [Settlements](/payments/settlements).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?">
    Yes, Dola requires a minimum balance before you can initiate a withdrawal. The exact threshold may vary depending on your region and chosen payout method. Your current balance and the minimum required amount are both displayed in **Account Settings → Payments**, so you always know exactly where you stand.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted for buyers?">
    Dola accepts major credit and debit cards as well as select digital payment methods. The available options are displayed at checkout and may vary depending on your location. All payments are processed securely — Dola does not store your full card details on its servers.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="My Agent is not responding — what should I do?">
    Start by checking the Agent's status indicator in **Dashboard → My Agents**. If it shows an error, try deactivating and reactivating the Agent. Also confirm that any required external connections or API keys the Agent depends on are still valid and haven't expired. If the issue persists, check the creator's listing page for known issues or contact them directly through the messaging feature. If the Agent remains broken for an extended period, you can report it to Dola support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my verification email — how do I resend it?">
    If the verification email isn't in your inbox after a few minutes, first check your spam or junk folder, as automated emails are sometimes filtered. If it's not there, return to the Dola login screen and click **Resend Verification Email** — you'll receive a fresh link shortly. Make sure the email address you signed up with is correct. If you still have trouble, contact Dola support and they can manually verify your account.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
