The Dola Marketplace is a curated storefront where AI creators publish ready-to-work Agents and digital products, and buyers discover, purchase, and activate them without any setup or deployment. Whether you're looking to extend what Dola can do for you or you've built something worth sharing with the world, the Marketplace is where supply and demand meet — instantly.

## What you'll find on the Marketplace

The Marketplace carries four types of products, each designed to let buyers get value quickly and creators earn revenue from their work.

## How it works for buyers

When you browse the Marketplace, you see each listing's description, sample outputs, creator profile, and buyer reviews before you spend a cent. Once you find an Agent or skill that fits your needs, purchasing and activating it takes less than a minute.

1. **Browse** — search by keyword, category, use case, or price range.
2. **Purchase** — check out securely using a supported payment method.
3. **Connect** — your new Agent appears immediately in **My Agents**. Click **Connect** to activate it and start working right away.

There's no deployment pipeline, no configuration file, and no waiting period. The Agent is live the moment you connect it.

## How it works for sellers

If you've built an Agent, assembled a powerful configuration, or developed a skill that others would find useful, you can list it on the Marketplace and start earning. Set your own price — one-time or subscription — and Dola handles the storefront, checkout, and delivery. Once your listing is approved, it's visible to every buyer on the platform.

Your Creator Dashboard gives you a live view of sales, ratings, and revenue for every listing you own. You can update, pause, or remove listings at any time without losing your historical data.

## The referral layer

You don't have to be a seller to earn on the Marketplace. Every Dola user — buyer or creator — gets a unique referral link. Share that link, and when someone makes a qualifying purchase through it, you earn a commission. It's a straightforward way to monetize your audience or community, even if you've never built an Agent yourself.
