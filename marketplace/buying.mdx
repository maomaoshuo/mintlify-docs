The Dola Marketplace lets you go from discovery to a fully working AI Agent in under a minute. Every listing on the Marketplace is a ready-to-activate product — no configuration files, no deployment pipeline, no technical expertise required. Find an Agent that fits your workflow, buy it, connect it, and start working.

## Browsing the Marketplace

Visit [dolaio.cn](https://dolaio.cn) and navigate to the **Marketplace** from the main menu. From there, you can explore the full catalog or narrow things down using search and filters.

- **Search** — type keywords related to the task you want to automate or the capability you're looking for.
- **Categories** — browse by topic or use case to discover Agents you might not have thought to search for.
- **Filters** — refine results by use case, underlying model type, price range, and creator rating so you only see listings that genuinely match your needs.

Before you buy, open any listing to review:
- **Sample outputs** — real examples of what the Agent produces
- **Creator profile** — background on who built the Agent and their track record on the platform
- **Buyer reviews** — honest ratings and feedback from people who have already purchased

## Purchasing an Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a listing">
    Click on any listing in the Marketplace to view its full detail page, including the description, sample outputs, pricing options, and buyer reviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy or try">
    Click **Buy Now** to proceed directly to checkout. If the listing offers a trial period, click **Try** to explore the Agent before committing to a purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Review your order and pay using one of Dola's supported payment methods. Your purchase is processed securely and confirmed instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your Agent in My Agents">
    As soon as your payment is confirmed, the Agent appears in **My Agents** in your dashboard. No waiting, no email confirmation required — it's there immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Open the Agent in **My Agents** and click **Connect** to activate it. This links the Agent to your Dola account and makes it ready to work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using your Agent">
    Your Agent is live. Start a conversation, assign it a task, or integrate it into your workflow — no additional setup is required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After your purchase

Once you've connected an Agent, your relationship with it doesn't stop at activation. The Marketplace is built so that buying an Agent gets better over time, not just at the moment of purchase.

- **Access anytime** — open **My Agents** from your dashboard to see every Agent you own, whether it's active, paused, or pending connection.
- **Full control over activation** — you can activate, pause, or adjust an Agent's settings at any point. Pausing an Agent doesn't cancel your purchase; you can reactivate whenever you're ready.
- **Automatic updates** — if the creator improves the Agent — whether that means upgrading the underlying model, refining the system prompt, or adding new capabilities — you receive the updated version automatically. You never need to repurchase to stay current.
- **Contact the creator** — if you have questions about the Agent or need guidance on using it effectively, reach out to the creator directly through the listing page.

## Tips for buying well

<Tip>
  Review the Agent's sample outputs and buyer ratings before purchasing. Sample outputs show you exactly what to expect, and a strong track record from other buyers is the best signal that an Agent delivers on its description.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Some Agents include an initial trial period that lets you test the product before committing to a full purchase. Look for the **Try** button on the listing page — if it's there, take advantage of it.
</Note>

<Note>
  Refund policies vary by listing and are set by each creator. Before you buy, scroll to the bottom of the listing description to review the creator's refund terms so you know where you stand.
</Note>
