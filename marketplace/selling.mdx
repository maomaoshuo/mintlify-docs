The Dola Marketplace gives you a global storefront for anything you've built or can offer. Once your listing is approved, every buyer on the platform can discover it — and every purchase goes straight to your earnings balance. Here's everything you need to know to list your first product and build a sustainable creator business on Dola.

## What you can sell

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI Agents" icon="robot">
    A fully built, ready-to-activate Agent with a defined purpose, system prompt, and toolset. This is the flagship product type on the Marketplace.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skills" icon="puzzle-piece">
    An individual capability — a discrete tool or integration — that buyers can add to an Agent they already own.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Agent Configurations" icon="sliders">
    A pre-set configuration of an existing Agent: your chosen model, instructions, and settings packaged as a reusable product. Great for niche workflows and industry-specific use cases.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personal Services" icon="handshake">
    Consulting, guided setup, custom builds, or any other hands-on help you offer. Services let buyers work directly with you rather than purchasing a digital product.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Listing process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finish building your product">
    Make sure your Agent, skill, or configuration is complete and working as intended before you create a listing. If you're still building, see the [Agent building guide](/agents/building) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open My Listings in your Creator Dashboard">
    Navigate to your **Creator Dashboard**, then select **My Listings** → **New Listing** to start the listing flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the product type">
    Select **Agent**, **Skill**, **Configuration**, or **Service**. Your choice determines which fields and settings appear in the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the listing details">
    Provide a clear title, a thorough description, screenshots or a demo video, relevant tags, and the most appropriate category. The more detail you give, the easier it is for the right buyers to find your listing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your price">
    Choose a pricing model: a **one-time purchase** or a **subscription** (monthly or annual). You can offer both options on the same listing if your product suits either arrangement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your payout method">
    Before you can receive earnings, add a payout method under **Account Settings** → **Payments**. Dola supports multiple withdrawal options — configure yours so you're ready to get paid from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    When your listing looks right, submit it for review. Dola's team checks every listing for quality, accuracy, and compliance with Marketplace guidelines. Most reviews are completed within a short turnaround period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Once your listing is approved, it's immediately visible on the Marketplace. Buyers can discover, purchase, and activate your product right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for selling well

<Tip>
  Add clear screenshots or a short demo video to your listing. Buyers want to see what they're getting before they commit — visual proof of your Agent in action is one of the strongest conversion drivers.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Write a detailed description that includes real use cases and example outputs. Specificity builds confidence. Instead of "a helpful writing assistant," show exactly what kind of writing it does and what the output looks like.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Respond quickly to questions from prospective and existing buyers. A fast, helpful response builds your seller reputation and directly influences your ratings — which in turn affect how prominently your listing appears in search results.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Dola deducts a platform fee from each sale. Your net earnings after the fee are always visible in your **Earnings Dashboard**, so you know exactly what you'll receive before and after each transaction.
</Note>

## Managing your listings

Your Creator Dashboard is the control center for everything you've listed on the Marketplace. From there you can:

- **Edit or update** any listing at any time — update the description, swap out screenshots, adjust pricing, or modify the underlying Agent. Buyers who have already purchased an Agent automatically receive any updates you push.
- **Pause a listing** temporarily if you need a break or are making significant changes. A paused listing is invisible to new buyers but remains intact for existing customers.
- **View per-listing analytics** including total sales, average rating, buyer reviews, and revenue over time — so you can see what's working and double down on it.
