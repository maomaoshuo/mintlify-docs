The Dola Referral Program lets anyone on the platform — whether you're a creator, a buyer, or both — earn a commission by sharing the Marketplace with others. You don't need to have built an Agent or sold anything to participate. If someone makes a qualifying purchase through your referral link, you earn a percentage of that sale, credited directly to your Dola earnings balance.

## How to find your referral link

Your unique referral link is available at any time from your account.

1. Open your **Profile** from the top navigation.
2. Select **Referrals**.
3. Click **Copy** to copy your unique referral link to the clipboard.

Share this link anywhere — in a message, on social media, in a blog post, or directly inside a listing description. Every click is tracked to your account automatically.

### What counts as a qualifying purchase

Any paid transaction completed through your referral link qualifies — including:

- **AI Agents** — one-time purchases or subscriptions
- **Skills** — individual capabilities bought through your link
- **Agent Configurations** — pre-set setups purchased via your link
- **Personal Services** — consulting or custom work booked through your link

### Referral attribution

When someone clicks your referral link, Dola records the attribution for a set window of time. If they make a qualifying purchase at any point within that window, your commission is applied. Check your **Referrals** dashboard for the current attribution window duration, as it may be updated periodically.

## Tracking your referral performance

Your **Referrals** dashboard gives you a clear picture of how your link is performing:

- **Clicks** — how many times your link has been opened
- **Conversions** — how many clicks resulted in a qualifying purchase
- **Commissions earned** — total commission credited to your balance, broken down by transaction
- **Pending vs. confirmed** — commissions that are awaiting confirmation versus those ready to withdraw

## Tips for earning more with referrals

<Tip>
  Share links to specific listings rather than the Marketplace homepage. A link that lands a buyer directly on an Agent they're already interested in converts far better than a generic entry point.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  If you're a creator, add your referral link to your Agent descriptions and to your social media profiles. Every time someone discovers your work and then explores the broader Marketplace, you have a chance to earn on whatever they purchase — not just your own listings.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Referral commissions are tracked and paid separately from your creator earnings. Both revenue streams appear in your **Earnings Dashboard** so you always have a complete picture of what you've made on Dola.
</Note>
