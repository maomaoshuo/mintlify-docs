Every sale you make on Dola contributes to your creator balance, which you can withdraw to your preferred payout method at any time once funds are available. This page walks you through tracking your earnings, setting up a payout method, and understanding how and when Dola settles your revenue.

## Tracking your earnings

Open **Creator Dashboard → Earnings** for a complete picture of your financial activity on Dola. The dashboard breaks your income down so you always know exactly where your money is coming from.

- **By listing** — see how much revenue each individual Agent or skill has generated.
- **By date** — filter earnings by time period to track monthly or annual performance.
- **By transaction type** — earnings are categorized as sale revenue, referral commissions, or creator bonuses, so you can see the composition of your balance at a glance.

### Pending vs. available balance

Newly earned revenue appears first as a **pending** balance. After a brief hold period — designed to account for potential refunds or disputes — pending funds move to your **available** balance, which is ready for withdrawal. Both figures are shown side by side in your Earnings dashboard so you always know how much you can withdraw right now versus how much is on its way.

## Setting up your payout method

You must connect a payout method before you can withdraw funds. Complete this setup as early as possible — ideally before you publish your first listing — so you're ready to receive earnings the moment your first sale comes in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Account Settings → Payments">
    In your Dola account, open **Account Settings** and select the **Payments** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Payout Method">
    Select **Add Payout Method** to begin connecting a withdrawal destination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your preferred method">
    Select the payout method that works best for you. Available options vary by region — the options shown are specific to your location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details and verify your account">
    Provide the required information for your chosen method and complete any verification steps. Dola may prompt you to confirm ownership of the account before activation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your payout method is now active">
    Once verified, your payout method is saved. All future withdrawals will be sent to this destination unless you update it in **Account Settings → Payments**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Set up your payout method before publishing your first listing so you're ready to receive earnings immediately after your first sale — no delay while you configure a withdrawal destination after the fact.
</Tip>

## Withdrawing funds

When your available balance is greater than zero, you can withdraw at any time directly from your Earnings dashboard.

1. Go to **Creator Dashboard → Earnings**.
2. Click **Withdraw** and confirm the amount you want to withdraw.
3. Dola sends the funds to your connected payout method.

Keep the following in mind when withdrawing:

- **Minimum withdrawal amounts** — a minimum threshold may apply depending on your region and payout method. If your available balance is below this threshold, the **Withdraw** button will indicate the minimum required amount.
- **Processing times** — after you initiate a withdrawal, funds typically arrive within **1–5 business days**, depending on your payout method and financial institution.

## Referral commissions

If you share your Dola referral link and another user makes a qualifying purchase through it, you earn a referral commission. These commissions appear in your Earnings dashboard under the **Referrals** tab and follow exactly the same settlement flow as your regular sale revenue — they accumulate as a pending balance, move to available after the hold period, and can be withdrawn the same way.

---

## Identity verification for payouts

<Note>
  Dola may require identity verification before processing payouts above certain thresholds, in compliance with applicable financial regulations. If verification is needed, you'll be prompted to complete it in **Account Settings → Payments**. Completing verification early prevents delays when you reach withdrawal thresholds.
</Note>
