Dola includes a built-in payment system that manages the entire commerce loop — from the moment a buyer completes checkout to the moment a creator receives their earnings. You don't need to integrate a third-party payment processor or build your own billing infrastructure; Dola handles collection, fee deduction, and settlement automatically so you can focus on building or using great Agents.

## How Dola payments work

Every transaction on Dola flows through the same pipeline: a buyer pays for an Agent or skill, Dola processes the payment, deducts the platform fee, and credits the remaining revenue to the creator's balance. This closed loop means both sides of the marketplace always have a consistent, predictable experience.

## For buyers

When you purchase an Agent or skill, you pay directly through Dola's checkout using a supported payment method. After a successful purchase you get immediate access to your new Agent.

- **Pay with supported methods** — credit and debit cards are accepted everywhere; additional regional payment methods appear at checkout based on your location.
- **Choose your purchase type** — listings are sold as either one-time purchases (permanent access) or subscriptions (monthly or annual access). The listing page always shows which model applies before you commit.
- **Manage your subscriptions** — open your **Billing Dashboard** to view active subscriptions, update your payment method, or cancel at any time.

## For creators

Dola tracks every sale automatically and makes your earnings visible in real time inside your **Creator Dashboard**.

- **Earn revenue from every sale** — each time a buyer purchases your Agent or skill, the sale revenue (minus the platform fee) is added to your creator balance.
- **View earnings at a glance** — your Creator Dashboard shows a full breakdown of revenue by listing, date, and transaction type.
- **Withdraw on your schedule** — once your balance is available, you can withdraw to your connected payout method whenever you're ready.

## Platform fee

Dola charges a percentage fee on each successful sale. The exact fee rate is always visible in your **Creator Dashboard → Listings** before you publish, so you can price your listing with full knowledge of what you'll net. Fees are deducted automatically; you never have to calculate or remit them manually.

## Covered initial traffic costs

To help newly published Agents gain traction, Dola covers initial traffic costs for recently purchased Agents. This means buyers can start using a new Agent right away without friction, giving your listing the best possible chance of earning positive early reviews.

## Purchase models

| Model | How it works |
|---|---|
| **One-time purchase** | Buyer pays once and retains permanent access to the Agent or skill. |
| **Subscription** | Buyer pays a recurring monthly or annual fee for ongoing access. |

You choose the model that fits your Agent's value proposition when you create your listing.
